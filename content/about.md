I am a first-year Mathematics student at the University of Toronto, St. George Campus. My research interests include: 

- Algebraic number theory
- Aperiodic tessellation
- Elliptic curves
- Game theory
- Holomorphic dynamics
- Low-dimensional topology
- Noncommutative algebra
- Olympiad geometry

Among other areas.

Prior to beginning my undergrad at the  University of Toronto, I competed in the FIRST Robotics Competition, reaching the regional finals with my team. I have also been involved in Model UN, theater and improv performance, and pure mathematics through the UofT Math Academy program.

I am a lifelong learner with broad interests across languages, technology, and the arts. I am a native speaker of English and French, and am currently studying Russian and Mandarin Chinese.
